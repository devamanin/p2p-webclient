However, Omegle permanently shut down in November 2023 due to growing safety concerns, legal challenges, and an inability to effectively moderate harmful content on the platform. Why Did Omegle Shut Down?

Omegle's closure was driven by a combination of factors. The platform faced numerous lawsuits related to child safety, as its lack of robust moderation made it a target for predatory behavior. The founder cited the constant battle against misuse as unsustainable. Omegle had no account system, no identity verification, and minimal content moderation — creating an environment where harmful behavior could thrive unchecked.

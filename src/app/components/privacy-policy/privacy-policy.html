This Privacy Policy describes how blynq.app ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera and Microphone Access

Our application requires access to your device's Camera and Microphone to provide video chat services.

  • Camera: We use the camera to capture your video stream during video calls.
  • Microphone: We use the microphone to capture your audio during video calls.

Video and audio data are transmitted in real-time between you and the person you are chatting with. We do not store these streams on our servers.

1.2 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Age and Gender (optional)
  • Location data (to match you with others nearby, if permitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate video and audio communication between users.
  • Manage your account and preferences.
  • Improve our services and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your profile name, age, and gender are visible to other users during matching.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services like Firebase (Google) for authentication and database management.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Manage or delete your account within the app's Profile settings.
  • Request account deletion through our website at: blynq.app/delete-account
  • Revoke Camera and Microphone permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

mail support@blynq.app

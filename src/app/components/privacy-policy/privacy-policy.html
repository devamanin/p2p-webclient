Last Updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how blynq.app ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera and Microphone Access

Our application requires access to your device's Camera and Microphone to provide video chat services.

  • Camera: We use the camera to capture your video stream during video calls.
  • Microphone: We use the microphone to capture your audio during video calls.

Video and audio data are transmitted in real-time between you and the person you are chatting with using peer-to-peer WebRTC technology. We do not store these streams on our servers. Your media is never recorded, saved, or archived by blynq.app.

1.2 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name: Your display name used within the platform.
  • Email address: Used for account authentication and important service communications.
  • Age and Gender (optional): Used to improve matching accuracy based on your preferences.
  • Profile photo: Optionally uploaded for your public profile.
  • Interests and preferences: Categories and topics you select to personalize your matching experience.

1.3 Location Data

If you enable our "Real Mode" feature, we collect approximate location data to match you with users near your physical location. This location data is:

  • Used solely for proximity-based matching and is never shared with other users.
  • Collected only when Real Mode is enabled and you have granted location permissions.
  • Stored temporarily and deleted when no longer needed for matching.

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the Service, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and browser information.
  • IP address (used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate geolocation).
  • App usage patterns such as session duration, features accessed, and interaction data.
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics to improve the Service.

1.5 Advertising Data

Our Service uses third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense and AppLixir, which may collect certain data for ad personalization and measurement purposes. This may include:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID).
  • IP address and general location for geographic ad targeting.
  • Interaction data with advertisements displayed within the Service.

You can manage ad personalization settings through your device settings or through Google's ad settings page.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate video and audio communication between users.
  • Manage your account, profile, and preferences.
  • Power our smart matching algorithm to connect you with compatible users.
  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service and its features.
  • Enforce our community guidelines and detect safety violations.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • Send you important service-related notifications (e.g., security alerts, policy changes).
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience and platform performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your profile name, age, and gender are visible to other users during matching. Your email address and exact location are never shared with other users.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services including Firebase (Google) for authentication and database management, and advertising partners for displaying ads. These providers process data only on our behalf and under our instructions.
  • Law Enforcement: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person, prevent fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • Safety Reports: When we report confirmed instances of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or other authorities, we provide relevant account information as required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Peer-to-peer WebRTC encryption for all video and audio communications.
  • Secure authentication powered by Firebase Authentication.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring for unauthorized access.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Chat messages are retained based on your disappearing messages settings.
  • Usage and analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymized form.
  • Video and audio call data is never stored.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information at any time through the app settings.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data through the app's Profile settings or by visiting blynq.app/delete-account.
  • Permissions: You can revoke Camera, Microphone, and Location permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Ad Preferences: You can manage ad personalization through your device or Google ad settings.

7. Children's Privacy

blynq.app is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete that information and terminate the associated account. Please refer to our Child Safety Standards for more information on our commitment to child safety.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and Service may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and authentication state.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns.
  • Deliver and measure the effectiveness of advertisements.

Third-party advertising partners may also use cookies and similar technologies to serve and personalize ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

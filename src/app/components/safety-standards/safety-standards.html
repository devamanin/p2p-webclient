At blynq.app, we are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all our users. We have a zero-tolerance policy towards child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

blynq.app strictly prohibits the creation, distribution, or promotion of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers children. Any user found to be engaging in such activities will be subject to immediate and permanent account termination.

2. Prevention and Moderation

We employ several layers of protection to prevent and detect child safety concerns:

  • Automated Scanning: We use industry-standard automated tools to scan for known child sexual abuse material.
  • Real-time Reporting: Our platform provides easy-to-use in-app reporting tools for users to flag suspicious behavior or content instantly.
  • Manual Review: Our dedicated safety team reviews all reports related to child safety with the highest priority.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

If you encounter any content or behavior that concerns you regarding child safety, please take the following actions:

  • In-App Report: Use the "Report" button available during video chats or on a user's profile.
  • Email: Contact our designated safety point of contact at amansinghpydev@gmail.com.

All reports are kept confidential and are investigated thoroughly.

4. Cooperation with Authorities

blynq.app complies with all relevant child safety laws. We report all confirmed instances of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with regional and national law enforcement authorities in their investigations.

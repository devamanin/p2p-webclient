Safety Standards & Community Guidelines

arrow_back Back

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

At blynq.app, we are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all our users. This page outlines our safety standards, community guidelines, and the measures we take to protect our community. Every user is expected to read and adhere to these guidelines when using the platform.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

blynq.app strictly prohibits the creation, distribution, or promotion of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers children. Any user found to be engaging in such activities will be subject to immediate and permanent account termination.

We also maintain a zero-tolerance stance toward:

  • Sexual exploitation or abuse of minors in any form.
  • Grooming behavior or solicitation involving minors.
  • Sharing or requesting explicit content involving individuals under 18.
  • Any content that sexualizes, endangers, or harms children.

2. Community Guidelines

All users of blynq.app are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that is respectful and considerate of others. The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

2.1 Harassment and Bullying

  • Harassing, intimidating, or threatening other users in any way.
  • Sending unwanted sexual content, advances, or messages.
  • Engaging in cyberbullying or repeated targeting of an individual.
  • Using derogatory language based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other personal characteristic.

2.2 Explicit and Inappropriate Content

  • Displaying nudity, sexually explicit content, or pornographic material during video calls.
  • Engaging in or broadcasting sexual acts during video chats.
  • Sharing violent, graphic, or disturbing content.
  • Promoting or glorifying self-harm, suicide, or dangerous activities.

2.3 Fraud and Deception

  • Impersonating another person or entity.
  • Catfishing or using misleading profile information.
  • Engaging in scams, fraud, or financial solicitation.
  • Spamming or promoting unsolicited commercial content.

2.4 Illegal Activities

  • Using the platform to facilitate or promote any illegal activity.
  • Distributing or promoting illegal substances.
  • Recording video calls without the consent of the other participant (where prohibited by law).

3. Prevention and Moderation

We employ multiple layers of protection to prevent and detect safety concerns:

  • AI-Powered Content Moderation: Our platform uses advanced AI models to analyze video streams in real-time and detect inappropriate content including nudity, explicit material, and other violations. When inappropriate content is detected, the video is automatically blurred and the user may be flagged for review.
  • Automated Scanning: We use industry-standard automated tools to scan for known child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Real-time Reporting: Our platform provides easy-to-use in-app reporting tools that allow users to flag suspicious behavior or content with a single tap during any video call.
  • Instant Blocking: Users can instantly block any other user, immediately ending all contact and preventing future matches with that individual.
  • Manual Review: Our dedicated safety team reviews all reports related to user safety with the highest priority. Reports involving child safety are escalated immediately.
  • Account Verification: Requiring account registration helps maintain user accountability and allows us to permanently ban violators from creating new accounts.

4. Enforcement Actions

Violations of these guidelines may result in the following actions, depending on the severity and nature of the offense:

  • Warning: A formal warning sent to the user's account for minor first-time violations.
  • Temporary Suspension: A time-limited suspension of the user's account for moderate violations.
  • Permanent Ban: Immediate and permanent termination of the user's account for severe violations or repeated offenses.
  • Legal Reporting: For violations involving child safety, illegal activities, or threats of violence, we report the incident to the appropriate law enforcement authorities.

5. Reporting Mechanisms

If you encounter any content or behavior that concerns you, please take the following actions:

  • In-App Report: Use the "Report" button available during video chats or on a user's profile. This is the fastest way to flag an issue and will immediately alert our safety team.
  • Email: Contact our designated safety point of contact at amansinghpydev@gmail.com for urgent safety concerns.
  • General Support: For non-urgent issues, you can also reach us at support@blynq.app.

All reports are kept confidential. We do not disclose the identity of the reporter to the reported user. Reports are investigated thoroughly, and we aim to respond to all safety-related reports within 24 hours.

6. Cooperation with Authorities

blynq.app complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations worldwide. We report all confirmed instances of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with regional and national law enforcement authorities in their investigations. We also comply with applicable data preservation and disclosure requests from law enforcement agencies.

7. User Responsibility

While we invest heavily in safety measures, a safe community is a shared responsibility. We encourage all users to:

  • Report any behavior that makes them feel uncomfortable or unsafe.
  • Never share personal identifying information (such as full name, address, phone number, or financial details) with people they have just met on the platform.
  • Be mindful and respectful in all interactions.
  • Review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service to understand their rights and obligations.
